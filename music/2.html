Subject: Music of vibecamp

I live in Guatemala. I will never be able to attend vibecamp. My musical dream is for someone to play one of my songs at vibecamp.

Anyway, vibecamp has music. It's even on the schedule:

```
Folk Dance Party (9pm-10pm) (Olympia)
Host: @dancinghorse16

Let's hold hands and dance! If you can walk forwards and backwards and count to 8, you can do this! We're talking good, ol' fashioned fun: music, movement and friends. Most dances have you changing partners every 40 seconds so it is a great chance to meet a lot of our fellow TPOT members. I'll teach you everything you need to know. Folks, this is LINDY. Please reach out to @dancinghorse16 to help instruct or DJ.
```
source: https://vibe.camp/schedule/
